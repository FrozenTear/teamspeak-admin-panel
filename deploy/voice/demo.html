TS6 ↔ WebRTC voice bridge demo

Joins a LiveKit room, publishes the browser microphone, and plays back any audio tracks the room subscribes you to. Pair this with a running ts6-voice-translator daemon to exchange Opus voice frames with native TS6 clients in the bridged channel. Acceptance: ≥30 seconds bidirectional audio between TS6 client + browser. See docs/voice-translator.md for the full recipe.
